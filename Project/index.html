LeftOverLove - Wasted food management platform



At LeftOverLove, we focus on making a difference through food waste solutions and animal care. Our goal is to reduce hunger by connecting surplus food with those who need it, supporting animal feeding programs, and promoting composting of organic waste. We believe everyone should have access to healthy food. Join us in our mission to create a better future for people and animals while caring for the planet.

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of your organization?

We aim to reduce hunger and promote sustainability by facilitating food donations, supporting animal feeding initiatives, and encouraging organic waste composting.

2. How can I donate food?

You can donate food by contacting us through our website or visiting our donation centers. We accept both cooked and raw food.

3.Do you accept animal food donations?

Yes, we gladly accept donations of nutritious food for animals in need. Your contributions help support our feeding programs.

4.What types of organic waste can I compost?

ou can compost fruit and vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, eggshells, and yard waste. Please avoid meat, dairy, and oily foods.

5. How can I get involved with your programs?

You can volunteer, donate food, or participate in our events. Check our website for more ways to help and make a difference!
